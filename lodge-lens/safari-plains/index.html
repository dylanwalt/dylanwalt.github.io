Equipment

Shot on the DJI Mini 5 Pro - a sub-250 g camera drone (249.9 g takeoff weight) from DJI's Mini series. It is one of the quietest options in its class, with refined low-noise propellers and a palm-sized foldable body - well suited to Safari Plains, where flights happen close to decks, bush dining and guest areas without the disruption of a larger aircraft.

For a lodge like this, that means discreet coverage of villas, waterholes and picnic setups, safer manoeuvring near trees and structures (omnidirectional vision plus forward LiDAR), and strong low-light performance at golden hour thanks to a 1-inch 50 MP sensor and f/1.8 lens.
