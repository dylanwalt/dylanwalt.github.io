# LawnPro Brand Brief

## Source truth

- LawnPro positions lawn care as specialist treatment rather than basic garden
  upkeep: the source compares the care standard to acclaimed golf courses.
- The central conversion promise is a free 14-point lawn analysis that determines
  suitable treatment for weeds, insects, diseases, lawn dressing, composting,
  seasonal maintenance, and product needs.
- Main service subjects in the scrape are lawn dressing, lawn weed treatment,
  instant lawn, scarifying or verticutting, spiking or aeration, mole cricket
  control, termite control, and winter lawn paint or dye.
- Seasonal maintenance extends the service story into fertilisation, pest
  control, garden composting, winter preparation, monitoring, and maintenance
  plans.
- Product routes cover lawn seedlings, seeds, DIY items, accessories, lighting,
  pest-related items, and garden utility products through a WooCommerce shop.
- Franchise content says the operating system has been tested since 1992 and
  franchising started in 2001. Treat those as source claims, not invented proof.

## Audience

- South African homeowners who want a visibly healthy lawn without learning the
  full diagnostic process themselves.
- Existing lawn owners with acute problems: mole crickets, termites, weeds,
  disease, compaction, winter browning, or disappointing growth.
- DIY buyers looking for seeds, seasonal products, and garden accessories.
- Prospective franchisees looking for a territory, training, equipment, systems,
  and an outdoor owner-operated business.

## Brand signals

- Logo: yellow field, deep green lettering, LawnPro treatment trailer mark, and
  a red medical cross in the final `O`.
- Useful phrases: lawn doctor, lawn analysis, grass from root to tip, high-grade
  products, seasonal maintenance, lawn care expertise.
- Imagery: real residential lawns, service close-ups, turf problems, seeds,
  garden products, the franchise trailer, and seasonal/winter assets.
- Tone: practical expertise with an aspirational outdoor payoff. It should feel
  exacting and optimistic, not sterile or cartoonish.

## Rebuild guardrails

- Keep the diagnostic posture visible. LawnPro should not read as a generic
  mowing or landscaping company.
- Do not fabricate certifications, service coverage maps, stock quantities,
  customer metrics, treatment guarantees, or ecommerce checkout behavior.
- Use local source images where they remain recognizable and honest. Improve
  the surrounding rhythm, hierarchy, crops, and motion instead of inventing fake
  before-and-after outcomes.
