# Brand Brief

South Side Soda is a premium beverage brand from South Africa with a focus on bold flavours, local ingredients, and a vibrant urban lifestyle.

## Tone
- Confident
- Energetic
- Authentic
- Refined street culture

## Audience
- Young adults 20-35
- Drinkers who value craft soda and local originality
- Customers looking for flavour-forward refreshment and bold brand personality

## Key brand pillars
- Refreshing, vibrant taste
- South African heritage
- Bold, colourful expression
- Premium quality with approachable edge
