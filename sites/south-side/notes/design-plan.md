# South Side Soda Rebuild Plan

## Goal
Create a premium one-page experience that positions South Side Soda as a bold, local craft soda brand with strong shelf appeal.

## Structure
1. Hero: bold headline, product highlight, CTA to shop or explore flavours
2. Why South Side: brand story, local craft, taste and quality
3. Flavour showcase: hero flavour cards, ingredients, sensory messaging
4. Product range: shop offerings with pricing cues
5. Social proof: rave review or retail placement
6. Footer: contact, socials, newsletter prompt

## Motion and interaction
- Use subtle Framer Motion reveals for sections and cards
- Keep animations smooth and reduced-motion friendly
- Use gradient and colour blocks for brand energy

## Experience principles
- Bold typography and clean layout
- Strong visual hierarchy with product imagery
- Short copy, strong CTAs, easy shopping path
