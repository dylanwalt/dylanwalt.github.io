# ClevaCado — Brand Brief

## One-liner
ClevaCado is a post-harvest diagnostic device that enters the avocado supply chain with the fruit and provides continuous telemetry to identify invisible bruising damage across every handling stage.

## The Core Problem
Avocado bruising is invisible until it is too late. By the time the bruise surfaces, the fruit has already failed the market — and the handling data that caused it is gone. There is no way to know which stage was responsible, so nothing can be fixed.

## The Solution
A single diagnostic device that:
- Travels with the fruit from field to market
- Captures 3-axis shock, vibration, and rotation data continuously
- Maps hotspots by processing stage
- Delivers a diagnostic answer: where, when, and why the damage happened

## Journey Stages (5)
1. **Harvest** — Field telemetry
2. **Bins** — Transfer telemetry
3. **Sorting** — Packhouse telemetry
4. **Transport** — Route telemetry
5. **Report** — Diagnostic answer
