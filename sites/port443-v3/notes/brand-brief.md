# Port443 — Brand Brief (V3)

## One-liner

Port443 automates cyber defence across the Middle East and Africa — control
attestation, unified posture visibility, custom SOC automation, and security
consulting for mid-to-large enterprises.

## Audience

- Enterprise security and IT leadership (CISO, CIO, SOC leads, security managers)
- Mid-to-large organisations needing continuous control validation, unified
  visibility across security/cloud/network estates, and automated response
- Regional buyers in MEA whose vendor shortlist mixes US category leaders and
  regional specialists; Port443 is positioned as the regional specialist with
  the engineering credibility of a category leader

## Primary CTA

- `Request a demo`

## Secondary CTAs

- `See OneView` (a deep link to the OneView product band)
- `Read the brief` (a download or anchor to the consulting overview)

## Offer / Pillars (do not invent claims)

1. **Control Attestation** — automated validations against vendor best practice
   and frameworks (PCI-DSS 4.0, NIST CSF 2.0, ISO 27001:2022, CIS v8).
2. **OneView** — governance-grade unified dashboard pulled directly from
   source technologies via API; trends posture with a clear RAG indicator.
3. **Custom Automation** — Identify → Scope → Build → Operate. SOC automation
   delivered as a managed SaaS lifecycle.
4. **Consulting** — exposure assessment plus automation roadmap, bridging
   security engineering and executive risk governance.

## Voice / Tone

- Confident, technical, enterprise-grade
- Calm and direct; never hype, never breach-theatre
- Bias toward evidence: numbers and frameworks are quoted exactly, never
  inflated or invented

## Proof / Trust Signals

- Operating across the Middle East and Africa.
- Press coverage on Ventureburn / TechFinancials (linked, not embedded as
  invented quotes).
- Framework alignment is the strongest single proof element — and is verifiable.
- No invented customer logos, awards, or numeric metrics.

## Visual constraints

- Source assets available: `hero-bg.jpg` (network photography),

- No first-party product UI screenshots have been provided. The rebuild

## Versions context

- **V1** — first pass, basic network hero and demo CTA.
- **V2** — cinematic command-centre direction (radar scan, telemetry feed,
  electric-mint accents on dark chrome). Strong atmospherically; over-indexed
  on cybersec-aesthetic clichés the category leaders deliberately avoid.
- **V3** — this build. Resets to **product-led editorial** in the manner of
  Wiz, Tines, and Linear. Trades atmosphere for product surfaces.
