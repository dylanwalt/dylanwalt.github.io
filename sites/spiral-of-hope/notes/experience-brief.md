# Spiral of Hope Experience Brief

## Thesis

Make Spiral of Hope feel like a textile spiral opening into community by using vibrant source table scenes, fabric-pattern bands, and collection reveals.

## First Viewport

- Brand signal: source wordmark, South African-made line, and saturated table-setting hero.
- Hero scene: blue table décor scene framed by a woven spiral motif.
- Next-section hint: collection swatches and maker-story proof enter below the hero.

## Sequence

1. Handmade collection hero and browse/reseller CTAs.
2. Source story strip about the pivot, local making, and hope.
3. Collection gallery with table, home, bags, and new designs.
4. Maker-process image rhythm.
5. Contact and reseller conversion.

## Signature Interaction

- Motion map: a spiral line rotates slowly behind the hero; fabric tiles reveal in bands; collection cards shift as swatches.
- Reduced motion: line becomes a still pattern and tiles fade without scale shifts.

## Assets

- Keep: source wordmark and table, collection, story, bag, and maker images.
- Replace: ecommerce/navigation chrome with collection storytelling and clear static route coverage.
