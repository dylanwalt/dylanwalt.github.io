# The Farm Barn Experience Brief

## Thesis

Make The Farm Barn feel like a lively farm stop rather than a store directory by turning its map, categories, and family add-ons into a country-market journey.

## First Viewport

- Brand signal: the source barn logo, Pretoria East line, and a panoramic illustrated route motif.
- Hero scene: farm map layered with feed, café, vet, and play-area windows.
- Next-section hint: an offer ticker runs from supplies to café.

## Sequence

1. Visit-oriented farm hero with call and directions CTAs.
2. Offer paddock showing animal feed, farm supplies, pet supplies, and hay.
3. Map and one-stop story.
4. Café, on-site vet, and kids-area beats.
5. Contact/hours panel.

## Signature Interaction

- Motion map: map layers rise with a gentle parallax; category chips sweep across a rail; offer images scale on hover.
- Reduced motion: no parallax, no rail drift, fixed category images.

## Assets

- Keep: source logo, illustrated map, and category/source convenience images.
- Replace: dense page-builder grids with a clear visit path.
