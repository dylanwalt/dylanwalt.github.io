# Port443 — Brand Brief (Draft)

## One-liner

Port443 builds cybersecurity software, automation, and integrations for enterprise customers across the Middle East and Africa.

## Audience

- Enterprise security and IT leadership (CISO, CIO, security managers, SOC leads)
- Mid-to-large orgs needing visibility, control validation, and automated response across security/cloud/network controls

## Primary CTA

- Request a demo

## Offer / Pillars

- Compliance & Governance: automated validations against best-practice + PCI, NIST, CIS
- OneView: unified view of critical info across security/cloud/network controls
- Community Defense: coordinated controls + automation for rapid containment via IOCs
- Consulting: exposure assessment + roadmap for mitigation

## Voice / Tone

- Confident, technical, enterprise-grade
- Clear and direct (avoid hype; prioritize credibility)

## Proof / Trust Signals (from homepage)

- “Operating across the Middle East and Africa”
- Links to coverage/news (Ventureburn / TechFinancials)
